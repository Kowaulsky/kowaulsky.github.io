Maison Chevalier Bot

← Back to Portfolio

A Discord bot developed in Node.js to automate management of the “Maison Chevalier” shop on the SchoolRP server: sales processing, client account management, shop opening/closing, and shift management.

Features

📥

Sales Processing

Automatic recording and logging of transactions in real time.

👥

Client Account Management

Create, update, and view client profiles linked to Discord/Minecraft.

🏪

Opening / Closing

Dedicated commands to start and end shop sessions.

⏱️

Shift Management

Start, end, and track staff shifts with centralized history.

💰

Points Management

Assign and remove client points for loyalty and promotions.

🛒

Stock Management

View, add, and remove items via an interactive interface.

The source code of this bot is private.
Available upon request for recruiters or in a professional context.
