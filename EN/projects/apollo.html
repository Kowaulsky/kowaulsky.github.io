Apollo

← Back to Portfolio

Apollo is a private alternative to Aircrack-ng developed in Go for performing legal Wi-Fi audits: handshake capture, packet injection, and advanced analysis.

Features

🔍

Scanning & Discovery

Analyze surrounding Wi-Fi networks and extract information (SSID, channel, signal strength).

📥

Handshake Capture

Record WPA/WPA2 exchanges for brute-force resilience testing.

⚙️

Packet Injection

Send deauthentication packets to target devices and force handshakes.

📊

Detailed Reports

Automatically generate reports with success statistics and analysis time.

🔒

Enhanced Security

Runs entirely locally with no uncontrolled external dependencies.

The source code of Apollo is private.
Available upon request for recruiters or in a professional context.
