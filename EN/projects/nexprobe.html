NexProbe

← Back to Portfolio

NexProbe is a network reconnaissance tool designed to automate information-gathering phases during penetration tests. Integrating Nmap, smbmap, and ldapsearch, it offers a streamlined workflow and real-time feedback to quickly assess infrastructures.

Features

🛠️

Centralized Integration

Orchestration of Nmap, smbmap, and ldapsearch from a single interface.

🤖

Smart Enumeration

Automatic correlation and analysis of results.

⚙️

Customizable Profiles

Adapt scans to specific needs (protocols, services).

⏱️

Real-Time Feedback

Monitor progress and receive alerts during scans.

📊

Detailed Reports

Generate clear reports including raw outputs and summaries.
